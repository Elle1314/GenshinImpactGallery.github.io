The Seven Nations of Teyvat

Seven nations ruled by one of the seven Archons, each whom controls one of the seven elements.

MONDSTADT

A city of freedom that lies in the northeast of Teyvat. Ruled by the Anemo God, Barbatos, now goes by the name Venti the Bard.

LIYUE

A bountiful harbor that lies in the east of Teyvat. Ruled by the Lord of Geo, Rex Lapis Morax, now called Zhongli.

INAZUMA

An Isolated Archipelago Far East of Teyvat. Formerly ruled by the Electro Archon, Beelzebul, now ruled by her twin sister, Baal, also called Raiden Shogun.

SUMERU

The city of scholars located in the west-central part of Teyvat. Formerly ruled by the Dendro Archon, Buer or Great Lord Rukhadevata, but now ruled by her successor, Lesser Lord Kusanali.

FONTAINE

Fontaine is one of the seven regions in Teyvat. It is the nation that worships Focalors, the Hydro Archon.
